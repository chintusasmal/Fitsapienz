Posterior Correction

Bad posture is a common problem for many people, as we live in a world full of activities leading to poor posture.Posture is not a position, but a dynamic pattern of reflexes, habits, and adaptive responses to anything that resists you being more or less upright and functional. Homo sapiens is naturally allergic to physical stress. Bad posture doesn’t only make you look unhealthy and unattractive, it also causes several health problems. Speed is defined as the ability to move the body in one direction as fast as possible. Agility is the ability to accelerate, decelerate, stabilize, and quickly change directions with proper posture.
