Bad posture is a common problem for many people, as we live in a world full of activities leading to poor posture.Posture is not a position, but a dynamic pattern of reflexes, habits, and adaptive responses to anything that resists you being more or less upright and functional. Homo sapiens is naturally allergic to physical stress. Bad posture doesn’t only make you look unhealthy and unattractive, it also causes several health problems. In addition, being quick with proficient speed and agility can build confidence that will lead to better performance in their sport.
